Spotify Favorites
CSV Downloader

If the only place where you have a complete list of your favorite music is Spotify, you should be looking into a way of backing up a list of all your favorite songs, artists, albums and whatnot. With how companies love to make their products shittier by the day, it is only a matter of time before you loose access to your music library due to some bullshit change of policies os something like that. Back up your music library. Or not, I'm not your mom.

I'm not gonna pretend this is a super impressive project to put on my portfolio or anything like that, it took me like 15 minutes to make and two more to get the actual CSV into my computer. The reason I decided to put it here is to remind you that your music library os not safe on the servers of a company and to give you an easy way to actually back up your music if you come across this article by chance.

Check the repo and give me stars here. A readme with instructions is also there if you want to use it.
