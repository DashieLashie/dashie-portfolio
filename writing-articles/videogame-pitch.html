Videogame Narrative Pitch
Singularity

This game concept has been on my mind for a while now, an outlet for me to explore big concepts like as history and human meaning. I'm not sure if I'm making justice to such big topics but my hope with this concept was to make an exploration on questions such as "What is the point of all this struggle?" and "How can we make conscious choices on the belief systems that we use to make sense of life?".

Before drafting this narrative pitch, I didn't really have much mechanics or gameplay thought out at all. However, as I worked on describing the characters and scenarios the picture of a parkour exploration game started to click for me. Most of the game happens in big, mostly abandoned ships with half forgotten stories and old characters trying to find meaning in a static, dead universe. So, having mechanics of movement and freedom contrasting themes of a frozen and chained-to-circumstance simply made too much sense.
